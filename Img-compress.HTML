How to Use Our Image Compressor

Our tool helps you reduce image file size while maintaining good visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean more compression)
  3. Select your preferred output format or use "Auto" for best results
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download the optimized image when processing is complete

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times (improves SEO ranking)
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Better user experience on mobile devices
  • Optimized storage space
